MCLAREN ARE 2024 CONSTRUCTORS' CHAMPIONS!

After years of rebuilding and fighting in the midfield, McLaren has finally returned to the top — winning the 2024 Formula 1 Constructors' Championship for the first time since 1998.

The team scored an incredible 666 points, with 6 Grand Prix victories, 21 podiums, 8 pole positions, and 7 fastest laps throughout the season. Their 2024 car, the MCL38, proved to be a game-changer — fast, reliable, and competitive on all types of circuits.

Lando Norris had the season of his life, winning 4 races and finishing 2nd in the Drivers' Championship with 374 points. Oscar Piastri also impressed, taking 2 wins and finishing 4th overall with 292 points. The duo consistently delivered double points finishes and front-row lockouts, helping McLaren beat Ferrari and Red Bull in one of the closest seasons in years.
